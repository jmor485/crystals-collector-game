Crystal Collector

Here's How The Game Works:


1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number you received at the beginning of the game.

4. You lose the game the game if your total score exceeds the random number value.


5. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
